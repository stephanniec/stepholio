Stephanie L. Chang is an M.S in Robotics candidate at Northwestern University.
She has a B.S in Bioengineering: Biotechnology from the University of California, San Diego.

Stephanie is passionate about creating robots which augment patient autonomy and enable users to explore the world in novel ways. She also has a research background in biomaterials fabrication and biosensor design. Prior to attending Northwestern, Stephanie was a full-time researcher and lab manager for Dr. Mark Mercola at Stanford University.
